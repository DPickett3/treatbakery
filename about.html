About

I'm DeAndria Pickett and welcome to my food blog. Each week I try out a new recipe and post it here to share it with the world. If you enjoy any of the recipes I post feel free to leave a comment.

I grew up in Jackso, Mississippi and learned to bake from my mother and grandmother. After finishing highschool I started baking full time and selling baked goods at local markets. Now I work full-time at a bakery in Brandon, Mississippi.
